Instructions

  1. Choose from the following poses and let the system know which pose you've chosen by saying the name of the pose
    • Tree Pose
    • Warrior 2
    • Plank
  2. The system will show you a photo of the pose
  3. Try to perform the pose such that the camera captures your body at a similar angle as the picture, what you do will be picked up by the system
  4. The system will provide feedback on adjustments you can make if necessary
  5. Try to make adjustments based on the system's recommendation
  6. If you cannot or don't want to make the adjustments the system is recommending, feel free to say "no"
  7. To start over, say "restart" or refresh the page
